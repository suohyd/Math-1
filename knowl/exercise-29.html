Exercise 9.

Consider the system of equations \(AX=B\) with \(A\in M_{m\times n}(\R)\) and \(B\in M_{m\times 1}(\R)\text{.}\)
  1. Show that if the above system has two distinct solutions then it has infinitely many solutions.
  2. Show that the above system has a solution in the complex numbers if and only if it has a solution in the real numbers.
