\begin{equation*} \begin{pmatrix}1\amp 1\amp 1\\a\amp b\amp c\\a^2\amp b^2\amp c^2\end{pmatrix} \end{equation*}
Find a condition on \(a,b,c\in\K\) so that the determinant of the above matrix is nonzero.
