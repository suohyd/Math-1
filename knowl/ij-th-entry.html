Observation 1.1.4.

Suppose that \(A\in M_{m\times n}(\mathbb{R})\) and \(B\in M_{n\times\ell}(\mathbb{R})\text{.}\) We denote the \((i,j)\)-th entry, i.e., entry in the \(i\)-th row and \(j\)-th column of the matrix \(A\) (respectively, \(B\)) by \(a_{ij}\) (respectively, \(b_{ij}\)). The \((i,j)\)-th entry of the matrix multiplication \(AB\) is given by the following formula.
\begin{equation*} \sum_{k=1}^{n}a_{ik}b_{kj},\quad\text{where}\quad 1\leq i\leq m\quad\text{and}\quad 1\leq j\leq\ell \end{equation*}
