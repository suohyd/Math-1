Exercise 12.

In each of the following system \(AX=B\text{,}\) the matrices \(A,B\) are given. Find the matrix \(X\text{.}\)
  1. \begin{equation*} A=\begin{pmatrix}-1\amp 0\\-1\amp 1\end{pmatrix}\quad \text{and}\quad B=\begin{pmatrix}0\\1\end{pmatrix}\text{.} \end{equation*}
    Find \(X=\begin{pmatrix}x_1\\x_2\end{pmatrix}\text{.}\)
  2. \begin{equation*} A=\begin{pmatrix}5\amp 2\\-2\amp 0\end{pmatrix}\quad \text{and}\quad B=\begin{pmatrix}7\\5\end{pmatrix}\text{.} \end{equation*}
    Find \(X=\begin{pmatrix}x_1\\x_2\end{pmatrix}\text{.}\)
