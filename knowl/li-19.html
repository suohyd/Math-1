Item 9.

For a matrix \(A\in M_{m\times n}(\mathbb{R})\text{,}\) the transpose of \(A\text{,}\) denoted by \(A^t\text{,}\) is an \(n\times m\) matrix obtained by writing columns of \(A\) as rows of \(A^t\text{.}\) Thus if
\begin{equation*} A=\begin{pmatrix}a_{11}\amp a_{12}\amp\cdots\amp a_{1n}\\a_{21}\amp a_{22}\amp\cdots\amp a_{2n}\\\vdots\amp\vdots\amp\ddots\amp\vdots\\a_{n1}\amp a_{n2}\amp\cdots\amp a_{nn}\end{pmatrix}\in M_{m\times n}(\mathbb{R}) \end{equation*}
then the transpose of \(A\) is
\begin{equation*} A^t=\begin{pmatrix}a_{11}\amp a_{21}\amp\cdots\amp a_{n1}\\a_{12}\amp a_{22}\amp\cdots\amp a_{n2}\\\vdots\amp\vdots\amp\ddots\amp\vdots\\a_{1n}\amp a_{2n}\amp\cdots\amp a_{nn}\end{pmatrix}\in M_{n\times m}(\mathbb{R}) \end{equation*}
In the following, given \(A\) compute its transpose and also compute \(A\,A^t\) and \(A^t\, A\text{.}\)
  1. \(A=\begin{pmatrix}a\amp b\amp c\end{pmatrix}\in M_{1\times 3}(\mathbb{R})\text{,}\) where \(a,b,c\in\mathbb{R}\)
  2. \(A=\begin{pmatrix}a\\b\\c\\d\\e\end{pmatrix}\in M_{5\times 1}(\mathbb{R})\text{,}\) where \(a,b,c,d,e\in\mathbb{R}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle A=\begin{pmatrix}1\amp 2\amp 3\\-1\amp -2\amp -3\\0\amp 0\amp 0\end{pmatrix}\in M_3(\mathbb{R})\)
  4. \(\displaystyle A=\begin{pmatrix}0\amp 1\amp 0\\1\amp 0\amp 0\\0\amp 0\amp 1\end{pmatrix}\in M_3(\mathbb{R})\)
  5. \(\displaystyle A=\begin{pmatrix}-1\amp 0\amp 0\amp 0\\0\amp 0\amp 0\amp -1\\0\amp -1\amp 0\amp 0\\0\amp 0\amp -1\amp 0\end{pmatrix}\in M_4(\mathbb{R})\)
  6. Find a condition on \(a,b,c,d\in\mathbb{R}\) such that the matrix \(A=\begin{pmatrix}a\amp b\\c\amp d\end{pmatrix}\) will be equal to its transpose.
  7. Consider a matrix \(A=\begin{pmatrix}a\amp b\\c\amp d\end{pmatrix}\text{.}\) Find a condition on \(a,b,c,d\in\mathbb{R}\) such that \(A\,A^t=I_2\) and \(A^t\,A=I_2\text{.}\)
